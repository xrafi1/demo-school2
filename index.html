Our Mission & Vision

Our mission at Appify School is to cultivate a vibrant and inclusive learning ecosystem. We are dedicated to fostering intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and a lifelong passion for learning in every student.

Our vision is to be a leader in education, recognized for empowering students to become compassionate, confident, and innovative leaders who can positively contribute to a rapidly changing global society.

Admissions

Join the Appify School family. We are excited to welcome new minds.

Our Admission Process

  1. 1

    Submit Inquiry

    Fill out the online inquiry form or visit our office to express your interest and receive a prospectus.

  2. 2

    School Tour & Interaction

    We invite parents and students for a campus tour and a friendly interaction with our admission counselors.

  3. 3

    Application & Assessment

    Submit the completed application form along with necessary documents, followed by a simple age-appropriate assessment.

  4. 4

    Admission Confirmation

    Successful candidates will receive an admission offer. Complete the fee payment to confirm the seat.

Academics

A comprehensive curriculum designed for 21st-century success.

Primary (I-V)

Focus on foundational literacy, numeracy, and scientific curiosity through play-based and interactive learning methods.

Middle (VI-VIII)

Transition to subject-specific learning, encouraging critical thinking, project-based work, and collaborative skills.

Secondary (IX-X)

Advanced, in-depth study aligned with the national curriculum, focusing on board exam preparation and career guidance.
